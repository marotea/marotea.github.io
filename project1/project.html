Venice

Africa

Venice is a place I have always wanted to visit. I created this piece, based off of images I found online, last year.

I created this piece on a day when "I bless the rains down in Africa" was stuck in my head. Was I trying to have the piece resemble Africa in any way? No, but that does not change the fact that I think of blessing rain down in Africa every time I look at it.
